304 GRAcn Boon Δ [1576-77

Conceditur 22 Martii vt magister Ricardus Spencer literas habeat gradus
et conversationis sui sigillatas sigillo vestro communi

Eadem gratia conceditur 22 J unii magistro Willelmo Goldingham

Conceditur 22 Martii vt doctores Perne et Harvy et magister J acobus Byll
sint iudices deligati in causa appellacionis per magistrum Thomam
Beaumond facte sic vt id ratum sit vestra auctoritate quod maior pars
eorum decreverit infra tempus per statutum limitatum (appellacio
forisfacta xx⁸ onary.)

Conceditur 24 Maii vt dominus Edw. Melton in artibus bacchalaureus et
Tho. Bainer clericus presententur domino Rogero North vt vnus eorum
promoveatur ad vicariam de Burwell iam vacantem per resignaoionem
Ovini Ducket vltimi incumbentis ibidem

Conceditur 5 J ulii vt expense facte et faciende per procuratores vestros
in negocio Sturbrigiensi allocentur per dominum procanceuarium
doctores Perne Hawfoni Stin et Binge aut per maiorem partem
eorundem

10 J ulye Maye it please yow to ratifye the stipend of xli lymited for
the keper of the librarye

10 Julye May it please yow yt John Kyngston latelye by yow chosen
to be one of the Stacioners and prynters of bookes in this vniuer-
sitie may have yo‘ lettres vpon this graunt scaled wth yo‘ common
seale -

15 Aug. Maye it please yow yt yor common seale may be set to the
quadripartite Indenture made between the ffcoffes of S* william
Pikeryng Sc Edward Wotton and Hester Wotton nowe reade before
yowe
